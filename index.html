The three most common failure modes

01

Reporting too low

Buried two levels under a single functional leader — never high enough in the org to be heard.

Fails · Total influence (#4)
02

Integrating from the exec layer

Alignment imposed top-down from committees instead of built where the data, process, and systems actually run.

Fails · Locus (#5)
03

Avoiding the hard fights

Smoothing over cross-functional disputes or forcing them by hierarchy — instead of surfacing and solving them.

Fails · Conflict resolution (#6)
